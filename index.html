Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevHub and who is it for? ‚Üí

DevHub is an all-in-one developer platform designed for individual developers, small teams, and growing startups. It combines project management, code collaboration, deployment pipelines, and analytics into a single seamless experience.

Can I switch plans at any time? ‚Üí

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated so you only pay for what you use.

Is there a free trial for paid plans? ‚Üí

Yes! Both the Pro and Team plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period so you can evaluate everything before committing.

What languages and frameworks are supported? ‚Üí

DevHub supports all major programming languages and frameworks including JavaScript, Python, Go, Rust, TypeScript, React, Vue, Angular, Django, Rails, and many more. Our plugin ecosystem extends support even further.

How does DevHub handle data security? ‚Üí

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. We perform regular security audits, maintain SOC 2 compliance, and offer two-factor authentication for all accounts.

What is the refund policy? ‚Üí

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all plans. If you're not satisfied with DevHub, you can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase. No questions asked.
